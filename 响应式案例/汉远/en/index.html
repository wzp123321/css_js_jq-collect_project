ABOUT US
Ningbo Hanyuan Lighting Co.,Ltd mainly specialize in LED outdoor lights of stainless steel at the beginning of 2004. With many years” experience in LED outdoor lighting, our company has formed a strong R&D team, powerful production capacity, strict quality control system and efficient marketing network. Hanyuan lighting not only equipped with advanced test machine such as LED light parameter tester, spectrograph and integrating sphere etc, but also built standard degradation testing room, anti-static workshop and standard assembling lines. Moreover , Our factory passed BSCI Third Audit.
